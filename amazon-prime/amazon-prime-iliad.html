Project Iliad
Amazon Prime Cancellation
Flow Redesign

Redesigning the Prime membership cancellation experience to create meaningful save opportunities - turning a single-page cancellation into a structured, empathetic retention journey across desktop and mobile.

Retention Design Conversion UX Mobile-First A/B Testing Prime Membership Save Flow
31.7M Members who cancelled Prime annually
330K USD estimated member yield from redesign
+18% Lift in saved memberships (1.9M → 2.2M)
5 Step redesigned flow vs. 4-step original

The Challenge

A Single Page with No Save Opportunity

The original Prime cancellation flow routed members directly from Prime Central to a simple confirmation modal - offering no meaningful opportunity to communicate value, address concerns, or offer alternatives. With 31.7 million free trial and paid members cancelling annually on Prime Central alone, there was a significant - and entirely untapped - save opportunity.

⚡

No Marketing Interstitial

Members could cancel in two clicks with no value reinforcement or messaging to reconsider the decision to leave.

💳

No Alternative Offers

Annual plan members had no path to switch to a more affordable monthly plan as an alternative to full cancellation.

📱

Inconsistent Mobile Experience

Mobile cancellation flow was not optimized for the device - critical messaging and CTAs were poorly structured for small screens.

🌍

Global Scale Challenge

The redesign needed to account for multiple regions - US, FR, DE, UK and beyond - each with different member volumes and regulatory considerations.

The Old Experience

Old Dive & Save Flow - 4 Steps, No Save

The original "Dive and Save" flow was a linear 4-step process that moved members quickly from Prime Central through a brief value messaging page directly to cancel confirmation - with no personalized offers, no alternative plan options, and no structured attempt to retain members.

The Redesigned Experience

Project Iliad - A Structured, Empathetic Save Journey

The redesigned flow added two new steps - a personalized Marketing Interstitial and an Offer Page - creating a 5-step journey that communicated Prime's full value, addressed concerns empathetically, and provided a meaningful alternative to full cancellation for annual plan members.

Marketing Interstitial (Winning Treatment) - Personalized, Multi-Benefit "[Member name], are you sure you want to end your membership?" - Personalized by name with three key benefit checkmarks: Free Two-Day Shipping, Unlimited Instant Streaming, Unlimited Music. Three clear CTAs: Remind Me Later, Continue to Cancel, Keep My Membership.
C. Offer Page - Monthly Plan Alternative "Before you go, consider switching to monthly payments." A Prime shield card offering $10.99/month with pro-rated refund details. Creates an exit ramp that prevents full cancellations for price-sensitive members.
D. Updated Cancel Confirmation Redesigned confirmation with clear refund amounts ($108.50) shown upfront for both "End on [Date]" and "End Now" options. Still offers Remind Me Later and Keep My Membership paths.
E. Cancellation Confirmed - Re-engagement Messaging "Cancellation confirmed." with refund timeline and an invitation to rejoin at any time. Benefits remain visible to encourage re-subscription - the journey doesn't truly end at cancellation.

The Impact

Significant Member Retention Lift

The Iliad redesign demonstrated measurable retention lift in initial milestones and established the playbook for Prime cancellation experience globally. Following US success, the flow was expanded to DE, UK, and additional international markets.
