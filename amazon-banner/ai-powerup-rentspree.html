The Challenge

Screening Was Broken, and Customers Knew It

Tenant screening was a known friction point in the RentSpree experience. Customers did not trust the process, and every delay in getting a clear result meant lost revenue for agents and landlords. The existing workflow relied on manual review, was error-prone, and created real compliance anxiety for landlords operating in heavily regulated markets.

🐌

Manual Reviews Were Slow

The screening process required agents and landlords to manually interpret dense reports, leading to delays in decision-making and lost applicants who chose faster alternatives.

📋

Agents Needed Clearer Results

Report outputs were hard to parse quickly. Agents needed instant, actionable insights, not raw data, to confidently recommend applicants to their landlord clients.

⚖️

Compliance Risk Was Real

Landlords were genuinely afraid of inadvertently violating Fair Housing laws. Without guidance built into the workflow, many avoided using premium screening features altogether.

"Tenant screening was a known friction point. Customers did not trust the process, and every delay meant lost revenue for them."

- Michael Storey, Head of Product Design and Research

The Solution

AI That Makes Screening Faster, Fairer, and Smarter

The AI capability was built on a foundation the team had already laid: customer obsession, a strong design system, and a culture of experimentation. The innovation did not start with technology; it started with listening to what customers needed most and working backward from trust.

Rather than bolting AI onto the existing experience, the team redesigned the entire screening workflow around intelligent assistance. AI auto-ranked applicants while embedding compliance guardrails directly into the decision flow. Landlords received clear, unbiased decision support at every step, removing the anxiety of making high-stakes decisions without guidance.

Three AI Capabilities
🤖
Intelligent Applicant Ranking
AI auto-ranks applicants against landlord criteria with built-in compliance guardrails to prevent discriminatory decision patterns.
🏘
AI-Generated Listings and Leases
Leases and property listings are auto-generated with tailored suggestions to enhance appeal, reducing creation time from 45 minutes to 5 minutes.
📝
Intelligent Communication Drafts
After approval decisions, the AI generates personalized acceptance letters and tailored messages for unsuccessful applicants, saving significant time.
01
List and Attract
AI generates the listing with benefit-led copy and compliance-ready content. Landlords can publish in minutes rather than hours.
02
Screen and Rank
Applicants are automatically scored and ranked. Compliance guidance is embedded at the point of decision, not in a separate policy document.
03
Decide and Communicate
One-tap conditional acceptance flows guide landlords through legal requirements. The AI then drafts personalized messages for all applicants.

The Process

Innovation Through Trust, Not Just Technology

This project succeeded because it was grounded in customer insight before a single line of AI code was written. The design team led discovery, built the trust architecture, and shaped the AI product's interaction model from the customer perspective outward.

Customer Trust + Compliance by Design + AI Intelligence = Revenue at Scale
Innovation does not always start with tech. It starts with listening and earning trust through results.
  • 1

    Deep Customer Research on Screening Pain

    Conducted qualitative interviews with landlords, agents, and applicants to understand the specific anxiety points in the screening process. Identified that compliance uncertainty was the primary barrier to premium screening adoption.

  • 2

    Map the Full Journey

    Mapped the end-to-end screening workflow for all three user types to identify where AI could remove friction, add intelligence, and build confidence. Created story-based scenarios to communicate the vision to engineering and business stakeholders.

  • 3

    Design Compliance Into the Interaction

    Worked with legal and product to embed compliance guidance directly into the AI-assisted decision flow. Designed the conditional acceptance flow so that landlords in regulated markets were guided through the correct process, rather than being warned after the fact.

  • 4

    Prototype, Test, and Iterate

    Built prototypes of the AI ranking interface and tested with real landlords and agents. Four months of iterative experimentation drove premium screening conversion from 19% to 47%. Each release was measured and the next iteration was designed around what the data revealed.

  • 5

    Extend AI to Listing and Communication

    Applied the AI capability to listing creation and applicant communication, reducing listing time on task by 90%. These features drove a 130% increase in listings created and earned recognition from industry awards.

The Timeline

Five Months from Problem to Proof

The AI power-up was designed and shipped in a focused five-month window, building on the organizational and product foundation established in the prior transformation. Speed was possible because the team already had the capability, culture, and customer insight to move decisively.

🔍
January 2024
Discovery: Understanding the Screening Anxiety
Conducted focused research sessions with landlords and agents to map the screening journey and identify the specific compliance and speed pain points blocking premium feature adoption.
🗺
February 2024
Journey Design and AI Architecture
Designed the end-to-end AI-assisted screening flow, including the compliance guardrail architecture and applicant ranking model. Story-based prototypes were reviewed with legal, product, and engineering before any build began.
🧪
March 2024
First Experiments: Screening and Ranking
Launched the first iteration of AI applicant ranking with a controlled user group. Initial conversion data showed a strong positive signal. Identified key friction points in the compliance flow and iterated rapidly.
🏘
April 2024
AI Listing and Lease Generation
Launched AI-generated listings and lease documents. Listing creation time on task dropped from 45 minutes to 5 minutes. Listings created increased by 130% over baseline. The team ran parallel experiments on communication drafts.
🏆
May 2024
Results and Recognition
Premium screening conversion reached 47% (from 19%). Screening revenue was up 50% YoY, with Q1 forecasts projecting 70% growth in the following year. The work was recognized with the AX100 Disruption Award and HousingWire Tech100.
