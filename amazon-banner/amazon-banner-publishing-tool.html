Self-Serve Banner
Publishing Tool

Transforming Amazon's marketing banner production by building a self-service design tool that enabled 54% of 325,000 annual design requests to bypass the creative team entirely - saving $10M USD in a single year.

The Problem

325,000 Requests. 181 Designers. Impossible Throughput.

Amazon Creative Services was handling hundreds of thousands of design requests for marketing banners annually. The sheer volume created a critical bottleneck: 2,098 marketers needed graphics, but only 181 designers could respond. The queue was unsustainable.

325,000
Design requests placed annually by marketers across Amazon
2,098
Marketers requesting banners, across global teams and categories
181
Designers available to respond - a ratio of 11.6 requests per designer per day
⏳

Design Queue Bottleneck

Marketers waited days or weeks for simple banner variations. Creative teams spent the majority of their time on low-complexity, templated work instead of high-value projects.

💸

Enormous Cost Structure

Every design request - regardless of complexity - flowed through the same expensive, time-consuming pipeline. Simple resizes and color variations cost as much as complex creative work.

🎨

Creative Talent Underutilized

Skilled designers were spending their expertise on templated banner production. Strategic and complex creative work suffered as a result.

The Solution

Amazon Designer: A Self-Service Creative Tool

The answer was to productize the most common design patterns into a self-service tool. Amazon Designer gave marketers the ability to create high-quality, on-brand banners themselves - without ever filing a request with the creative team.

📋

Template Library

Continuously updated library of templates across multiple banner types: TCG, ROTO, Stripe, Square, Large HQP, and more - covering every common marketing use case.

⚡

Quick & Simple Interface

Marketers fill in structured text fields, select a color theme, upload or choose a product image, and see a real-time preview. No design skills required.

🔗

Automatic Media Central Upload

Generated graphics were automatically uploaded to Amazon's Media Central system - eliminating the manual handoff step entirely.

📦

Batch Generation

Multiple images across different templates could be generated in one session - critical for campaigns needing many size variants of the same banner.

The Experience

A Professional Design Interface, Built for Non-Designers

The tool's UX challenge was significant: create an interface powerful enough to produce production-ready marketing assets, but simple enough that a marketer with no design training could use it in minutes. Amazon Designer achieved this through structured inputs, real-time preview, and guardrails on text length and formatting.

Full tool interface: template selector, text input fields with character limits, color picker, real-time canvas preview

🖊️

Structured Text Inputs

Title, subtitle, CTA, and extra copy fields each enforce character limits - ensuring every generated banner meets visual standards automatically.

🎨

Color Theme Selection

Pre-approved color themes (Dark Blue/Teal, and others) keep all output on-brand while giving marketers enough flexibility to match campaign needs.

👁️

Live Canvas Preview

Marketers see their banner update in real time as they type. Product images are positioned in a dedicated image area, maintaining consistent visual hierarchy.

🔄

Multi-Template Generation

Switch between template types (ROTO, Stripe, Square) without re-entering content - a single creative brief generates every size variant automatically.

The Impact

$10M Saved. 54% Self-Serve. Creative Team Transformed.

By intercepting the most common design request types, Amazon Designer fundamentally changed the economics of creative production at Amazon. In 2019, more than half of all global design requests were fulfilled without any designer involvement.
